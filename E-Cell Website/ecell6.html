About Us

We are group of highly enthusiastic individuals with a mission to inculcate the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youth. With an aim to guide the finest technical innovations from NIT Patna, to vibrant business concepts, we provide them with the necessary support and resources to establish them as the country's next ground breaking start-ups. They are networked to incubators, seed funds and angel investors all over the world, so to create successful entrepreneurial stories which will continue to inspire the coming generations of young entrepreneurs.
