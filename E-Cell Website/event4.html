B-Plan









The secret of business is to know something that nobody else knows.

                                                            Aristotle Onassis

With the mission to promote new ideas and to emulate the process of the growth of an idea towards a full-fledged startup. B-Plan prides itself on providing a 360 degree, holistic experience.Right from acknowledging that your idea has potential, to writing a B-Plan, and pitching in front of an investors this platform has it all !

The trademark of B-Plan, the business track of B-Plan is aimed at helping ideas develop into businesses that have the potential to revolutionize the world.

So,go on creating ideas!

This event consists of three rounds in which contestants may participate in a team of maximum 3 persons.

First Round:

  • Submit your abstract in the format provided here
  • Submit the extract on or before the last submission date.
  • The abstract submitted after the deadline will not be considered.
Second Round:
  • The participants selected in first round will have to present their ideas in the form of ppt. before the judges.
  • The time limit for one team is 10 minutes.
  • Be as specific as you can!
  • 10 teams will be selected from second round on the basis of their presentation.
Third Round:
  • The participants selected in second round will face a questionaire round.
  • The judges will ask the participants a series of questions on the basis of their presentation.
