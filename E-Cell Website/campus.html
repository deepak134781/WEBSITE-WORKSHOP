CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAMME

The E-Cell of NIT Patna is a non-profitable unit formed under the student welfare programme of NIT Patna that aims at appreciating and supporting the innovative ideas of young students from every corner of our country.To implement this ideas E-Cell NITP is going to select a STUDENT AMBASSADOR from all colleges to promote our vision.

     Our CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAMME has been started with an aim to achieve above visions.One student from each college will be selected as a College Ambassador to represent E-Cell NIT Patna in their college. More than one ambassador may be selected depending on the strength of college.


Selection Criteria:

  • To apply for Student Ambassador students have to send their resume to us on this given e-mail address ecellnitpatna@gmail.com
  • Selection will be on the basis of resume and telephonic interview.
  • Only one student will be selected from each college as a ambassador.
  • But more than one ambassador may be selected if strength of college is large.

Benefits:

  • Students will get a Certificate of Appreciation for being a part of events or workshop organized by E-Cell.
  • Students will get a chance to interact with student ambassadors of other colleges.
  • They will get a platform to express their ideas.
  • They will get a chance to participate and publicise entrepreneurship related activities on their college campus.
  • This will improve their creative skills to design their own entrepreneurship based events in their college in collaboration with E-Cell NIT Patna.
  • This will provide them an opportunity to improve their communication skills as they will interact with student ambassadors of other colleges.

Duties:

  • They have to promote our events or workshops in their colleges.
  • They have to put posters of our events and workshops in their college campus sent by us.
  • They have to coordinate with us in organizing our events.
  • They have to focus on the participation from their college.
  • The certificate of appreciation will be provided on the completion of term.
