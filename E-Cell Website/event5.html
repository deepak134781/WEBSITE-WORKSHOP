E-Quizzo









A good idea generates when one has a good deal with market.

				                     Ranjan Kumar

This event is designed with an aim to improve your knowledge about market.We are interested to acknowledge you about the great entrepreneurs and their achievements together with the current happenings in the world.

The main objective of this event is to test your decision making skills by some situation based questions.

This event consists of two rounds in which individual participation will be there.

First Round:

  • This round will consist of 50 objective questions.
  • The questions will be about the current ins and outs in the market.
  • Each question will have four options out of which only one will be correct.
  • Top 20 participants will be selected from this round for the next round.

Second Round:

  • This round will consist of situation based questions.
  • Participants will have to answer in the specified no. words.
  • Participants will have to use their own ideas and knowledge.
